Note-taking methods and effects on STEM and non-STEM students

This research project was conducted as part of my Research and Technology course of my Sophomore of Kean University. I was initially inspired to begin this research project because I wanted to assess if there is a connection between a students confidence in class and how they take notes. GMIS 2022 Research

Image Abstraction from the center of boundary balance

The purpose of this research project is to make a new method of motion tracking, and code was provided to start it. The program took an input of binary images and created an output image of the object's outline and another image with the object's outline and an estimated object skeleton both on a coordinate plane. The program uses different mathematical concepts to perform the math required to find an object's center of gravity and output its skeleton and outline. I contributed to this research by solving code errors and leaving comments to explain what each component of the code does. Furthermore, I took the results of running the program, and did a literature review of similar research to create a research Poster and corresponding Abstract describing my research, its results, and why it is significant. Furthermore, because this code and its results will eventually be used to train a transformer, I started the code transformation to python. This research project is what i presented at GMIS 2022, click the link to view my extended abstract.
